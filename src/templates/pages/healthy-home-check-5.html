5. Draught Stopping

A draught free home costs less to heat and will keep your tenants warm and happy. Become a draught detective and keep the breeze outside. As a rule of thumb, gaps or holes with a width greater than 3mm that let air into or out of the home will usually require blocking. This means that if the edge of a New Zealand $2 dollar coin can fit in the gap, then the gap needs to be sealed.

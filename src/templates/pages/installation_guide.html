What comes in your Mānawa sensor kit?

  • Mānawa gateway

  • 4 x Mānawa sensors

  • 1 x Micro USB to USB power cable

  • 1 x LAN cable

Turn on your Mānawa gateway

Plug in the Micro USB, into the slot next to the On/Off button. Plug in the USB into your router or use an adapter (not included) to plug it into a power socket. Press the On/Off button. Your Mānawa gateway will light up into a rainbow.

Connect your Mānawa gateway to the Internet

Connect the LAN cable to your Mānawa gateway. Connect the LAN cable to your router.

Login to Manawa.nz

Open your browser, go to https://manawa.nz/ then enter your username and password

Connect your sensors to your Whare

Making sure the gateway is on and while watching the front of the sensor. PRESS the button on the back for 3 secs, the sensor will long flash blue. PRESS AGAIN for 3 secs, the sensor will flash 3 times, your sensor is now ON It will take 10mins for the first reading from the sensor to show on your dashboard.

Name your sensor

On your dashboard, your sensor will be called “room-sensor-” with 12 characters. In the top right corner is a pencil, select this and change the name of your room and select the room type

Install your Mānawa sensors

Install your sensors in rooms, the lounge, kitchens, bathrooms any where you would want to know the healthiness of your home. We suggest installing at head height when sitting in the lounge or when lying in bed. We do not suggest installing the sensors high up, as heat rises making the reading higher than it should be. The 3M sticker may tear wallpaper or paint if pulled. Bluetack is acceptable to use as well.
